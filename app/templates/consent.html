Informed consent for an online experiment

You are invited to take part in a short online psychology study. Before you decide whether to participate, please read the following information.

Purpose. The purpose of this study is to collect responses to a brief judgment task.

Procedure. If you agree to participate, you will see two people, make one binary choice, and then answer one rating item.

Duration. The study takes about one minute.

Voluntary participation. Your participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time by closing the browser window.

Risks and discomfort. No substantial risks are expected beyond those of everyday computer use.

Data. Your responses, along with study session identifiers, may be stored for research and pilot-testing purposes.

Contact. If you have questions about the study, please contact the research team running this pilot.

Consent. By continuing, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old, have read this information, and agree to participate in this online study.
